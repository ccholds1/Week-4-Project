Joy Theatre

New Orleans: Where Magic Happens

The Joy Theater is a theater and historic landmark built in 1947 on Canal Street in downtown New Orleans, Louisiana. Renovations in 2011 transformed the former movie palace into a multi-purpose theater for live music, stand-up comedy, private functions, and corporate events. The theater’s iconic marquee sign was restored, as was the building’s original art deco architecture.
