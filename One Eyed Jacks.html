One Eyed Jack's

Where Words Fail, Music Speaks.

The bar and music venue One Eyed Jacks at 615 Toulouse St. has a storied history, from its days as the Toulouse Theatre (when James Booker played piano in the cast of a musical) to its turn-of-the-millennium incarnation as the Shim Sham Club, which established its rep as downtown's primary venue for local and touring rock, punk and other alternative acts. One Eyed Jacks has been its tenant for a decade, and continues to up the ante with hip buzz bands and occasional intimate presentations of big names on the music scene. The showroom in the back, which features flocked crimson wallpaper, vintage nudes on black velvet and a horseshoe-shaped bar, boasts some of the best sightlines in the city; the raked floor, recalling the club's past as a theater, means there's not a bad view in the house.
