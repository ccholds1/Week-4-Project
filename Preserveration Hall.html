Preservation Hall

Music. Reality. Sometimes It's Hard to Tell The Difference.

Preservation Hall was established in 1961 to preserve, perpetuate, and protect traditional New Orleans Jazz.[1] Operating as a music venue, a touring band (the Preservation Hall Jazz Band), a record label, and a non-profit organization, Preservation Hall continues their mission today as a cornerstone of New Orleans music and culture. Situated in the heart of the French Quarter at 726 St. Peter Street, Preservation Hall presents intimate, acoustic New Orleans jazz concerts nightly featuring some of New Orleans' finest performers, showcasing a musical legacy dating back to the origins of jazz itself.
